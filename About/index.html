Our Beginning

How it all started — and where it's headed.

10 January 2024
AcademeForge is Founded
A small Telegram community for Class 10 students solving doubts, running quizzes, building a community. Founded by Devraj Kumar at 17.
Mid 2024
Platform Vision Expands
From a Telegram group to a full learning ecosystem — app interface, student dashboard, test portal, and skill tracks conceived and built.
2025 — Present
Growing & Building
Certificate portal, analytics, leaderboards, and community features launched. Focus on practical education that closes the gap schools left open.
Goal: 100,000 Students
The 10-Year Vision
Reach every motivated, curious student regardless of geography, background, or school quality. Equal access to digital skills for all of India.
02

What We Believe

Our manifesto — deeply held.

01
Students are creators, not just consumers
Every student has potential to build something real. We shift the identity from passive learner to active creator — someone who makes things, solves problems, and ships work.
02
Practical beats theoretical, always
You don't learn to swim by reading about water. Our curriculum is built around projects, execution, and hands-on experience — not slides and definitions.
03
Geography shouldn't limit ambition
A student in a small town in Bihar deserves the same world-class skill education as anyone in a metro city. Location is not destiny. We're building for everyone.
04
AI is not a threat — it's a superpower
We teach students to work with AI, not fear it. Those who understand and use AI intelligently will have an enormous advantage in every field they choose.
05
Community accelerates everything
Learning alone is slow. Learning in a community of motivated peers — where you share, discuss, fail, and grow together — is exponentially faster. That's why AF Nexus exists.
