# AcademeForge: India's All-in-One Learning Ecosystem

**AcademeForge** is a growing Indian EdTech platform built to give students — especially young learners — a single space to study, track progress, test knowledge, connect with peers, and even manage their time. It blends affordable courses, AI tools, gamification, and productivity apps into one tightly connected ecosystem.

---

## 📚 Core Learning Platform

AcademeForge offers three distinct learning formats, designed to work for different kinds of students.

### 📖 Courses — Text-Based Learning with Certificates

The **Explore** section hosts AcademeForge's text-based courses. All lessons are **read-and-learn** — no audio or video required. This makes them genuinely inclusive, especially for students who are hard of hearing or prefer reading over watching. Once a course is completed, a **certificate is issued instantly**.

Available courses include Creative & Graphic Design Mastery, Video & Media Editing Mastery, AI Coding & Logic Foundation, Freelancing & Monetization Roadmap, and AI & Prompt Engineering. Students can track individual progress and access all earned certificates from a personal **Dashboard**.

### 🎬 LearnSpace — Video-Based Lecture Programs

**LearnSpace** is AcademeForge's video lecture library, available under the Programs tab. Students watch structured, instructor-led video lessons and progress through topics at their own pace.

Current LearnSpace programs include:

- **Freelancing, Client Work & Digital Monetization** — Online earning, client communication, service packaging, pricing basics, and digital skill monetization.
- **AI & Prompt Engineering** — AI tools, prompt design, structured AI workflows, content automation, productivity systems, and professional generative AI use cases.
- **Video Editing & Digital Media Production** — Professional video editing, short-form content creation, storytelling, audio sync, captions, and digital media workflows.
- **Creative Graphic Design & Brand Visuals** — Graphic design fundamentals, poster creation, thumbnails, banners, typography, branding, and visual communication.
- **AI Coding & Logic Foundation** — Coding logic, problem-solving skills, AI fundamentals, prompt thinking, and beginner-friendly programming concepts.

## 🤖 AF ProGuide — AI Student Mentor

**AF ProGuide** is AcademeForge's built-in AI mentor. It runs multiple AI model versions (AF 2.4, Dev 1.3, Tapri 7.9) and is designed to help students with:

- Learning to code (e.g., Python)
- Building a portfolio
- Preparing for job interviews
- Choosing a tech career

Students can type any question in a clean chat interface. A free tier is available, making it accessible without payment.

---

## 🎮 AF Test Arena — Gamified Learning & Quizzes

AcademeForge turns studying into a game through the **AF Test Arena**. Students take timed quizzes on topics like AI, Generative AI, Machine Learning, Digital Literacy, Computer Basics, and Communication Skills.

The gamification system includes:

- **XP points** earned for correct answers
- **Levels** (e.g., Level 5 = Silver rank)
- **Win streaks** and daily challenges (e.g., "4x4 Hard Sudoku — +250 Bonus XP")
- **Global Rankings** — Live leaderboards showing XP, wins, and streaks across all players

**Brain Games** like Sudoku are also built in under the Games section, keeping learning engaging and competitive.

---

## 🔥 Learning Heatmap — Streak Tracking

The **Learning Heatmap** encourages daily engagement. Students log in daily to maintain and grow their streak, which is tracked visually in a week-by-week activity grid. Missing a day resets the streak to zero — a simple mechanic that builds consistent study habits.

## 👥 Community

AcademeForge has a built-in community feed directly inside the app. Students can:

- Post thoughts, questions, or updates
- Like and comment on posts
- View stories from other members
- Send direct messages (Chats tab)
- Follow the official AcademeForge account for announcements

Posts support both English and Hindi/Hinglish, reflecting its Indian student base.

---

## ⏰ Timezy — Time Management App (by AcademeForge)

**Timezy** is a standalone productivity web app created under the AcademeForge umbrella. It's 100% free and browser-based, designed to help users:

1. **Add tasks** — Title, description, priority level (High/Medium/Low), and due date
2. **Plan the week** — A visual calendar planner highlights busy and free days at a glance
3. **Track and complete** — A live progress ring fills as tasks are marked done

Timezy supports both **Dark and Light Mode** and requires no account or download.

**Zenopulsky** is AcademeForge's content publishing arm. Positioned as "Independent · Unfiltered · Necessary," it publishes long-form articles and journalism on:

## 🔵 Capacity — Private Wellbeing App

**Capacity** is a quiet, private app for sharing how you're doing with a small, trusted group of people. Users set a simple emotional state — *Good, Okay, Stretched, Need Space, Off the Grid* — and their close circle can see it. It also includes a private group chat, keeping the experience intentionally small and low-pressure. It's separate from the Community and focused on emotional check-ins rather than content sharing.

## Who Is AcademeForge For?

AcademeForge is built primarily for **Indian students** — from school learners to young adults looking to build real-world digital skills. Its mix of structured courses, AI mentorship, gamified tests, community, and free productivity tools makes it a one-stop learning environment that goes beyond just video lessons.

With text-based courses accessible to all (including students who can't hear), instant certificates, an AI mentor, gamified quizzes, and a built-in community, AcademeForge is clearly building toward something much larger than a typical course platform.
